## How to Install on Mobile:

### **iPhone/iOS:**
1. Open Safari browser (must use Safari, not Chrome)
2. Navigate to the app URL or open index.html
3. Tap the Share button (square with arrow)
4. Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen"
5. Tap "Add" - the app icon will appear on your home screen
6. Open from home screen for full-screen app experience

### **Android:**
1. Open Chrome browser
2. Navigate to the app URL or open index.html
3. Tap the menu (3 dots)
4. Tap "Add to Home screen" or "Install app"
5. Confirm - the app icon will appear on your home screen
6. Open from home screen for full-screen app experience

## Hosting Options:

To access from phone, you need to either:

**Option A - Free Hosting:**
1. Go to https://vercel.com or https://netlify.com
2. Drag and drop your snoooo folder
3. Get a live URL to open on your phone

**Option B - Local Network:**
1. On your PC, run: `python -m http.server 8000` in this folder
2. Find your PC's IP address: `ipconfig` (look for IPv4)
3. On your phone (same WiFi), open: `http://YOUR_PC_IP:8000`

**Option C - GitHub Pages:**
1. Upload to GitHub repository
2. Enable GitHub Pages in settings
3. Access via the provided URL

Once installed, the app works **offline** and feels like a native app!
